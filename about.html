About me

Hey!


I'm Tomáš Kostrzeva — a graphic designer, creative coder & art director focused on building visual identities as systems rather than static outputs.

My work lives at the intersection of design, structure, and experimentation — balancing bold visual clarity with flexible logic and intuitive usability.

I work closely with brands, studios & agencies that value thoughtful design, innovation, and visual systems designed for scale.

How I work

I design visual identities by defining rules, relationships, and behaviors before shaping final outputs.

Using creative coding, I build visual systems that enables variability while keeping the identity coherent across different touchpoints.

This approach often leads to tailor-made brand tools
— interactive applications that help teams use and apply the identity with clarity and control.
